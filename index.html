Empyrean Rifts

      "Where the heavens fracture, destiny awakens."


Genre: Action-Adventure
Platform: PC (Unreal Engine 5.8)
Tone: Dark fantasy, cosmic horror

Inspiration: Rifts Megaverse, JoJo’s Bizarre Adventure, Fallout series, and Star Fox

Overview

Empyrean Rifts Gameplay Demo is a third-person superhero-style flight battle through the astral plane, alongside a mysterious and ancient emissary. Developed for PC on Unreal Engine 5.8, the game fuses the exhilarating freedom of superhero flight with gravity-based abilities. It’s a teaser of the full Empyrean Rifts game, allowing players to demo a slice of the ultimate powerset. It introduces the game’s “big bad”, the Vampire King, while withholding the protagonist’s ultimate secret (their identity as the Galaxy Soul incarnate) as a narrative hook for the full game.

Story

The Soul Sphere, an ancient cosmic artifact binding all realms, sits in a crystalline structure in the Astral Plane, guarded by astral beings. The Vampire King, disguised as an emissary of the desperate, warns the player of an impending invasion, tricking the player into destroying the Soul Sphere. This scatters fragments of the Sphere across realms, tearing open rifts in the fabric between the realms. These rough-edged wounds act as windows (and gateways) into other worlds. Through a rift in the desert, the player will see the purple-sky mushroom realm; another rift bleeds fiery light from Tartarus. The player must recover the fragments, repair the sphere, and banish the Vampire King. Along the journey, they discover the source of their power: They are the soul of the galaxy incarnate.

The initial Gameplay Demo starts with the player entering the Astral Plane and meeting an emissary who warns of an impending invasion into the Earth Realm. They must fly through the Astral Plane, fight waves of astral beings (using their innate gravity based attacks/powers), and destroy the portal to the Earth Realm. The Gameplay Demo concludes with the Vampire King exposing his true plan: using you to destroy the Soul Sphere, freeing him from Tartarus so he can spread his dominion over the Earth Realm.

Player Mechanics

Mechanic

Category

Description

Flight

Movement

Superhero flight: left stick/WASD for horizontal movement, space/shift or bumper buttons for elevation control, and right stick/mouse for camera aiming.

Kinetic Punch

Ranged Attack

Fire a line trace with heavy physics impulse along the camera’s directional vector.

Singularity

Area of Effect Attack

Create a single point of gravity, pull objects/enemies into the center. Implode/destroy anything that reaches the center before the event ends.

Core Gameplay Loop

  1. Fight astral beings.
  2. Destroy the portal to the Earth Realm.
  3. Win or be knocked out of the astral plane.

Level Design (Astral Plane)

The Astral Plane is a galactic, flowy cosmic corridor thick with drifting star-matter, luminous nebulae, and soul-currents. Movement here feels weightless and fluid. There is a destructible crystalline structure off in the distance. The player must destroy it to complete the Gameplay Demo.
