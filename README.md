# Improvements to the GDD from V1.0.0 -> V2.0.0

## 1. Too many agents
- ✓ Removed marketing agent and soundtrack agent for gameplay demo.

## 2. Better win condition
- ✓ Keeping it nice and concise, define it as: reach the crystalline fortress, destroy it, and defeat the boss enemy within. This triggers the cutscene showing the vampire king destroying the soul sphere and revealing his true motives.
New copy for core gameplay loop:
Battle waves of astral beings as you carve your path to the crystalline fortress.
Shatter the fortress and defeat the boss within to unlock the final cutscene.
Win, or be knocked out of the astral plane, forcing you to restart the wave.

## 3. Well defined fail-state
- ✓ If the user's health meter (call it a “Lucid meter”) reaches 0, they'll be knocked out of the astral plane, and faced with a short cutscene of going back to reality, and then re-entering the astral plane. Upon continuing they'll start at the beginning of the same wave in which they were knocked out.

## 4: Tone description:
Change "cosmic horror" tone to "cosmic fantasy adventure"
- ✓ Changed to "Cosmic Fantasy Adventure". And changed all references of cosmic-horror to cosmic-fantasy.

## 5: Leveling confusion, remove the "level 22" part.
- ✓ Removed the LVL 22 nonsense, and added a footnote explaining the video is just inspiration. I thought it was baked into the video (generated by Gemini), but in actuality it was some nonsense that Claude Design had added when it converted my Google Doc markdown into the beautiful html!

## 6 and #7: Communicating progression:
- ✓ There will be waves of enemies, after a set number of enemies are destroyed (adjustable based on gameplay testing) the user will "zoom/warp" forward, through a "soul current" (a nebular cloud ring), the crystalline structure will get closer and more centered, and the emissary companion will audibly say things like, "we're getting closer!", or "it looks like this is the last wave!" <- indicating the last wave.

## 8 and #9: Defining attacks and power meter:
- ✓ The kinetic punch attack and the singularity attack shall have icons in the HUD, that show the button they're are associated with. No cooldowns, but there'll be a circle around the singularity icon, it fills (clockwise from top) as the player inflicts damage on enemies (and also as time elapses). When the singularity is full/ready, then the user can use it.
The singularity event lasts 3s (to be adjusted after testing).

## 10: Defining Enemy types:
- ✓ Three: ranged attacker, melee attacker, and 1 boss. Give small descriptions of each, generate a little video for each

## 11: Stage the end cutscene:
- ✓ Explicitly stated it will be a cinematic cutscene at the end of the demo.
