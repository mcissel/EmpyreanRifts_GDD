01 — Overview

Severity Breakdown

One stacked bar per reviewer plus an all-reviewer aggregate. Toggle the lens above to compare Round 1 severities against the post-cross-examination Final severities — the aggregate row gained more BLOCKING weight after Round 2.

02 — Cross-Examination

Reviewer Agreement Matrix

Row = reviewer who wrote the Round 2 response ("from"). Column = the colleague they engaged ("to"). Fill opacity = volume of engagement; border style/color = whether the engagement was conflict-majority (amber, dashed) or connection-majority (grey, solid). Click a cell for the full argument text.

03 — Movement

Finding Flow — Round 1 to Final

Every one of the 30 findings as a ribbon from its Round 1 severity bucket to its Final severity bucket, coloured by the Final severity. Bent, rightward-climbing ribbons are findings the board judged worse after cross-examination; flat ribbons held their ground.

04 — Centerpiece

Top 5 Issues

Ranked by severity × confidence per the Round 3 synthesis. Click a card to expand its full cross-examination trail.

05 — Escalation

Unresolved Disagreements

Conflicts from Round 2 the board could not settle. Positions are shown as written; use "Show who agrees" to see how many reviewers stand on each side.
